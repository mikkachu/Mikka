C++ Programs!

Mikka has put her HEART AND SOUL into her C++ projects. Working longgggg hours to get them done. Dedication is an understatement in regard to her programming. Despite the endless challenges and arguments she's had with her computer, along with her computer's critical and unfair attitude towards her, she's managed to get the work done.

The C++ Final Project (top image) was by far the most challenging and complex. Mikka coded Tic Tac Toe where the user could play up to 5 games while having their scores recorded. They could also choose to play with another player or against the computer. The program includes random assortment, arrays and strings.

Mikka's Vending Machine (middle image) was quite successful. The vending machine sold poisons: Cyanide, botulinum, batchrotoxin to name a few. Like the Final Project, the Vending Machine was very long indeed. Mikka spent many hours on the project in order to make it the best it could be. If you are looking for lethal poisons, some with the potential of killing via respiratory failure and severe motor skill impairment, the Vending Machine is right for you.

The Scary door project (bottom image)(spoiler alert) includes no way of winning (the best result possible is getting stuck in a hole). The possible outcomes include getting stucked into a black hole, eaten by dogs, killed by robotic pigs while being poisoned by sulphuric acid, getting stuck in a hole, electrocuted by a metaphysical sphere or getting stuck in a metaphysical sphere. Overall, the punctuation graphics and program itself turned out well.

Despite the computer's disliking of Mikka, Mikka really enjoyed C++ and hopes to use it in her future career.
