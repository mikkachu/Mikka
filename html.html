HTML/CSS Programs!

Mikka really enjoyed composing all the websites the class did. She did her first website on quantum entanglement along with the following particles:

  • The Higgs Boson
  • Pions
  • Kaons
  • Quarks

She included a table of the types of quarks and their associated spins and symbols (on the right) along with 3 images.

While working on the first website, Mikka spent quite a bit of time browsing through different colours and experimenting with what colours go well together and which one's don't. The first website is predominately dark gray with grass green here and there, however as the first website was in progress she decided that her portfolio website was going to be white with a few hints of colour.

The first image on the right is the style sheet of the first website. The h3 tag provides the subheadings; the body provides the content and menu; and the img is simply an image on the website. I've added emphasis to some word using colour with the em tag and have included a hover feature for one of the lists in the website. The bottom image is a picture of a table I created about the types of quarks (quarks being elementary particles composing protons, neutrons, mesons and other quark-combinations). The 3 columns in the table represent the name, spin and symbol of the quarks. For example, row 2 states: Charm | 1/2 | c meaning that charm is the quark's name, 1/2 is its spin and its symbol is c. There are 6 types of normal-matter quarks therefore there are 6 rows not including the name-spin-symbol row.
